American Staffordshire Terrier

Polu loves to carry really big sticks and dig in true Terrier fashion.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Lifespan: 12-16 years
  • Height: 17-19 inches (at the shoulder)
  • Group: Terrier Group

The American Stafforshire terrier is a loyal companion. They are quite smart and require a lot of obedience training and careful attention for the first 2-3 years. Bred to be muscular and compact, yet they maintain a significant athletic prowess. The dubbed "Amstaff" or "Staffy" head is very robust and thus they require raw bones or toys for chewing.
